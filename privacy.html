RedlineRoom

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

RedlineRoom (“the app”) is made by Andrey Tyurin (“we,” “us”). This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your information. It is written to be read, not to be hidden behind legalese.

The short version. RedlineRoom has no account and no login. The only things that ever leave your device are cropped images of a markup you transcribe (the markup itself plus a small area of the sheet around it — never the whole sheet), a random device identifier that is not tied to who you are, and — if you subscribe — Apple’s signed confirmation of your subscription. We do not sell data, run ads, or track you. Any photo you attach to an item stays on your device and is included only in exports you choose to share.

What we send off your device, and to whom

When — and only when — you transcribe a markup (circling a detail and writing a note next to it sends that markup automatically), the app sends:

  • A cropped image of that one markup region (the loop and its handwritten note), together with a second, slightly wider crop of the sheet area immediately around it that helps the AI read your note in context. The surrounding crop is bounded in the app itself: if it would cover more than half the page, it is not sent at all — neither image is ever the whole sheet. Both go to our transcription service at redlineroom-api.cpmtek.com, which forwards them to Anthropic’s Claude API (model claude-opus-4-8) to convert your handwriting into text.
  • A random device identifier (a UUID generated on your device and stored in the iOS Keychain) used only to count your free transcriptions and to verify an active subscription.

If you subscribe, the app also sends your device identifier together with Apple’s signed subscription confirmation to our service — at purchase, at app launch, and when connectivity returns — so the server knows your device is Unlimited. This confirmation contains no name, email, or payment details.

That is the entire outbound data flow.

What we never send

  • Your full drawing or PDF — only the cropped markup region and its bounded immediate surroundings (never more than half of one page).
  • Your project names, file names, or notes other than the markup being transcribed.
  • Your location, name, email, or any account information — there is no account.
  • Any contacts, microphone, or advertising identifiers.

Photos you attach (optional)

You can optionally attach a photo of a condition to a punch item, using your camera or your photo library. These photos stay on your device. They are included only in a PDF you choose to export and share, and are never sent to our transcription service or to Anthropic. The camera or photo library is accessed only at the moment you add a photo.

Retention

  • The cropped images are used to produce text and are not retained on our servers after the response is returned.
  • Anthropic processes the image under its commercial API terms to generate the text and does not use API inputs or outputs to train its models. See Anthropic’s API terms: anthropic.com/legal/commercial-terms.
  • The random device identifier is stored only to enforce the free-transcription cap and subscription status. It is not linked to your identity and is not used to track you across apps or websites.

Subscriptions and payments

Subscriptions are sold and processed by Apple through the App Store. We never receive or store your payment details. Purchase and restore are handled by Apple’s StoreKit; we only receive Apple’s signed confirmation that a subscription is active, to unlock unlimited transcriptions.

Tracking

We do not track you. The app contains no advertising SDK, no analytics SDK, and no third-party trackers. It does not present an App Tracking Transparency prompt because there is nothing to track. NSPrivacyTracking is false and there are no tracking domains.

Children

RedlineRoom is a professional tool and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Your choices

Because there is no account and we retain no cropped images, there is no personal profile to access or delete. If you never transcribe and never subscribe, nothing leaves your device — drawing, the punch list, and PDF/CSV export all work fully on-device with no network use. To manage or cancel a subscription, use Settings → Apple Account → Subscriptions on your device.

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy we will update the date above and post the new version at this URL before the change takes effect.
